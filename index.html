About the Event

Where Kolkata meets
Open Source

Open Source Con Kolkata is a community-driven, all-day conference dedicated to the open source ecosystem. We bring together developers, contributors, students, and enthusiasts from across West Bengal and beyond.

From beginner workshops to deep technical talks, from networking to project showcases — this is your stage to learn, share, and collaborate in the true spirit of open source.

Whether you're a seasoned contributor or just starting your journey, you belong here.

FAQ

Got questions?
We've got answers

When and where is Open Source Con Kolkata? +
The exact date and venue are yet to be announced. Subscribe to our mailing list or follow us on social media to get notified the moment it's revealed!
Is the event paid? +
Open Source Con Kolkata is a paid, community-driven event designed for developers and students to explore open source, attend curated sessions, and network with peers.
How can I speak at the event? +
We have an open Call for Proposals (CFP). Click the "Submit Your Talk Proposal" button and share your idea. We welcome talks, workshops, and lightning talks on any open source topic.
Can my company sponsor the event? +
Absolutely! We have multiple sponsorship tiers. Download our sponsorship deck or reach out directly to discuss custom packages that fit your goals.
Can my community partner with Open Source Con Kolkata? +
Yes! We love partnering with local and national tech communities. Community partners get co-promotion, booth space, and more. Reach out to us via our social channels or email.
Is this event beginner-friendly? +
100%. We have a dedicated student and beginner track. Whether you've never contributed to open source or you're a veteran maintainer, there's something for everyone at this conference.
